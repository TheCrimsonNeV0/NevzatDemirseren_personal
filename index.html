About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Texas State University. My research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), with a particular emphasis on interaction techniques in Virtual Reality (VR). I also bring prior research experience in information assurance and data science, including the application of machine learning methods to health-related projects. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from TOBB University of Economics and Technology and my Master's degree in Computing and Information Sciences from the University of North Florida. These academic and research experiences provide me with a strong interdisciplinary foundation that informs my current doctoral work and long-term research objectives.
